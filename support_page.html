Guest Assistant Support Page

This is the support page for the Guest Assistant iOS app. The app allows users to login using the Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google options. If a user is not already authorized, they will be redirected to a "Request Access" page. Once authorized, the user can use the app to open/close the garage and control the music on the rooftop.

If you're facing any issues while using the Guest Assistant app, we're here to help.

Our support team is available 24x7 (lol!) to assist you with any queries or concerns you may have. We strive to provide our users with the best possible experience and are always looking for ways to improve.

Thank you for choosing Guest Assistant!
